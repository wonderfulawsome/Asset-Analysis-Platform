Passive Financial Data Analysis is a FastAPI-based investment analytics platform deployed on Railway. It collects market data from multiple external sources (Yahoo Finance, FRED, CNN, CBOE, Shiller), processes it through ML models (HMM, XGBoost, ensemble forecasting), stores results in Supabase, and serves them via REST API. A background scheduler (APScheduler) runs light pipelines every 10 minutes and full pipelines every 3 hours.
